Welcome to the

Architecture is an art form that mirrors how we project ourselves across the earth’s landscape, and, like other expressive mediums, it changes with styles, technologies and cultural adaptations. But their values remain. The works, in the material form of buildings, are often perceived as cultural symbols and as works of art through its aesthetic value and its semantics and relations with development of culture.

As the very first architectural NFTs, we designed 2000 unique high quality artworks based on the most acclaimed architectural creations in the world.

Derivative drawings of these eminent architectures, designed in the form of Pop Art pieces in vibrant colorful grids, are minted as non-fungible tokens and stored on the Ethereum blockchain. We chose from a a series of selected masterpieces by Antoni Gaudi, Le Corbusier, Ludwig Mies van der Rohe, Frank Lloyd Wright, IM Pei, Norman Foster, Zaha Hadid, etc. The depicted works include, but are not limited to, Chapelle Notre-Dame du Haut, German Pavilion (Barcelona Pavilion), Centre Pompidou, Salk Institute, La Sagrada Familia and Robie House, which have natural collection and art values.

Welcome to the Club
