Archiverse (ARKVRS) is the very first and largest #NFT collection of architectural pop-art pieces, launching in 2022.

The First and Largest NFT Collection of Digital Architecture

Become an early adopter of world-renowned architecture in 3D mode

We designed 4,000 unique high quality artworks based on the most acclaimed architectural creations in the world. You can mint those artwork as non-fungible tokens and store them on the Ethereum blockchain.

Join Our Club

Owners of our NFT artwork are our club members. Besides 3D assets, owners can also request services of customizing digital architecture in metaverse. The mission of the Archiverse project is to build a comprehensive digital architectural ecosystem.

Member Benefits

  1. Turn world-renowed architectures into your digital asset
  2. Win your free 3D assets
  3. Secure your spot in future airdrops
  4. Display your owned architecture with AR/VR
  5. Build your own house with member discount

Check our product roadmap and stay tuned for more benefits in the future!

Inspired by Masterpieces

We selected masterpieces designed by globally recognized architects - Antoni Gaudi, Le Corbusier, Frank Lloyd Wright and more. They include but are not limited to La Sagrada Familia, Chapelle Notre-Dame du Haut, Guggenheim Museum, and more. Each of them has a natural collection value which will increase over time.
